# Pediatrics

## Leading Causes of Death in Newborns

- Congenital anomalies
- Small for gestational age (SGA)
- Sudden infant death syndrome (SIDS)

- Necrotizing enterocolitis (NEC)
- Cerebral palsy

- Retinopathy of prematurity

## VACTERL Association {VACTERL}

- Vertebral anomalies
- Anorectal malformation
- Cardiac anomalies :: ventricular septal defect (VSD)
- Tracheoesophageal fistula (TEF)
- Esophageal atresia (EA)
- Renal anomalies
- Limb anomalies

## Presentation of Potter Sequence {POTTER}

- Pulmonary hypoplasia
- Oligohydramnios
- Twisted face
- Twisted skin
- Extremity defects
- Renal failure

## Presentation of DiGeorge Syndrome {CATCH22}

- Cardiac anomalies :: tetralogy of Fallot (TOF)
- Abnormal facies
- Thymic aplasia
- Cleft palate
- 1° Hypoparathyroidism → Hypocalcemia
- 22q11 microdeletion

## Presentation of Kawasaki Disease {F-SCARE}

- Fever :: > 40°C for > 5 days
- Strawberry tongue
- Conjunctivitis
- Adenopathy :: neck
- Rashes :: desquamating
- Edema :: limbs

## Common Skin Rashes in Neonates

- Erythema toxicum
- Seborrheic dermatitis :: cradle cap
- Diaper rash
- Cherry angioma
- Mongolian spots
- Milia

  - Subdural hemorrhage
  - Retinal hemorrhage
