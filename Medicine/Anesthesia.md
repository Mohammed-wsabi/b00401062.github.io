# Anesthesia

## Triad of Anesthesia

- Hypnosis
- Analgesia
- Muscle relaxation

## Contraindications of Neuraxial Blockade

- Increased intracranial pressure
- Infection
- Coagulopathy
- Patient refusal
- Hypovolemia
- Stenosis of mitral valve
- Stenosis of aortic valve
