## Presentations of Preeclampsia

### Preeclampsia

- BP > 140/90 mmHg
- Proteinuria :: urine protein ≥ 0.3 g/d
- Proteinuria :: urine protein/creatinine ratio (UPCR) ≥ 0.3
- Proteinuria :: dipstick ≥ 1+

### Severe Features

- BP > 160/110 mmHg
- Creatinine ≥ 1.1 mg/dL
- Disseminated Intravascular Coagulation (DIC)
- Edema
- Epigastric pain
- Headache
- Hemoconcentration
- Liver enzymes ↑
- Thrombocytopenia :: platelet count ≤ 100,000/µL
- Visual disturbances

## Indications of Intrapartum Prophylaxis Against GBS

- Fever
- History of GBS-affected neonates
- Preterm labor
- Rupture of membranes > 18 hours
- Urine culture
- Vaginal culture
