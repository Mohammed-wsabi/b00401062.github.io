## Ventilation/Perfusion (V/Q) Ratio

|Ratio|Conditions|
|-|-|
|0|Shunt|
|Low|Lung base|
|High|Lung apex|
|∞|Dead space|

### Shunt

- Anatomic shunt
	- Intracardiac shunt
	- Pulmonary arteriovenous malformation (AVM)
	- Hepatopulmonary syndrome
- Physiologic shunt
	- Atelectasis
	- Pneumonia
	- Acute respiratory distress syndrome (ARDS)
	- Obstructive lung diseases

### Dead Space

- Pulmonary embolism
- Pulmonary vascular diseases
- Obstructive lung diseases

## Modified Wells Criteria for Pulmonary Embolism

- Signs/symptoms of DVT
- PE is most likely clinical diagnosis
- Tachycardia
- Immobilization
- Previous PE/DVT
- Hemoptysis
- Malignancy
